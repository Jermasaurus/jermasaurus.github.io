Hello, and thanks for taking a look at my page!
My name is Jerry Sommerfeld and I’m a fourth year Computer Engineering and Computer Science student at Michigan Technological University. I have a wide variety of experience in the Aeropace industry, and I'm always looking for fun hardware projects to work on.

Currently I am working on three satellite missions ( Oculus, Auris, and Stratus ) with the Michigan Tech Aerospace Enterprise. In addition to enterprise project work, I’ve worked on a couple of hobby projects, such as a Spectrum Analyzer that uses fans to blow styrofoam beads in accordance with music frequencies. These and other projects can be found over at my Portfolio page.

In my free time I like to experiment in the world of audio. You'll usually find me digging around sound libraries, tweaking my surround sound setup, or building an amplifier out of whatever is on hand. Sound design projects are in the works, so stay tuned...

More information about past work experience can be seen over at my LinkedIn page. If you need to get in contact, you can do so there, or by sending me an email here.

Thanks for stopping by, and happy hacking!
